    Fees & Payment

    Registration Fee

    Due to the potentially large number of registrants, parents are reminded that the registration fee, $550.00 USD for each child, must be submitted with the fully completed Registration Forms or the student will not be registered. After June 28, the fee for each Junior Camp student is $600.00 USD.

    Incomplete forms will be returned and the student will not be registered until all of the completed forms and the fee are received. (Paying the fee before sending the forms does not hold a spot.)

    If paying by check, the check (payable to “ROEA”) must be submitted with the form. Please write the name of the camper in the memo section. Your application will not be reviewed until the check is received.

    Credit / Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Discover, American Express) are accepted online via Zeffy. Please include the camper’s name in the notes section. This link is only for Junior Camp.

    How It Works

    The Application Process

    1. Complete the online application — camper & parent/guardian together, with signed health forms attached to the Google form.
    2. You will receive an email from Camp Vatra confirming receipt of your completed application.
    3. Submit payment through the secure online link (or by check made out to ROEA).
    4. You will receive an email confirming receipt of payment.
    5. The Camp Vatra application will then be reviewed.
    6. You will receive a final acceptance email confirming attendance at Camp Vatra for Juniors.

    Please note: the only Health / Examination Forms that will be accepted are the ones included in the Registration Packet. No other forms (from school, etc.) will be accepted.

    Stay Connected

    We make an effort to provide updates, photos and other information via our Camp Vatra Facebook page (facebook.com/CampVatra), which we invite you to watch and “Like.” A Facebook account is not needed for basic viewing. Please be patient — photos during camp may not be posted every day.
