Our Hierarchs

Ruling and Vicar Hierarchs

Archpastoral Leadership

The Episcopate is led by His Eminence Archbishop Nathaniel, assisted by His Grace Bishop Andrei as Vicar Bishop.

N
Ruling Hierarch

Archbishop Nathaniel

Archbishop of Detroit and the Romanian Orthodox Episcopate of America. Long-serving archpastor leading the diocese with steadfast care for its parishes, monastic communities and youth ministries.

A
Vicar Bishop

Bishop Andrei

Bishop Andrei (Hoarște) was ordained Auxiliary Bishop of the Romanian Orthodox Episcopate of America on Saturday, February 1, 2025.
